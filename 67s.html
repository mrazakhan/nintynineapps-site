67s — number pattern puzzles

A brain puzzle game for quick, satisfying mental workouts.

Can you spot the pattern? Swipe across the grid to uncover hidden number sequences before the clock runs out. 67s is a fast-paced number puzzle game that challenges your pattern recognition and mental math skills.

Key features

🔢 Swipe to solve

Scan the grid and swipe across adjacent numbers to reveal arithmetic sequences, geometric progressions, Fibonacci, primes, perfect squares, cubes, triangular numbers and power sequences.

🧠 Train your brain

Sharpen pattern recognition, mental math, and strategic thinking. Start with simple "add 2" sequences on a 4×4 grid and work up to expert-level 5×5 boards with 8+ pattern types.

⏱️ Race against the clock

Every round is timed — find all hidden sequences before the countdown hits zero. Finish fast to earn time-bonus points.

💡 Hints & Peek

Stuck? Use Hint to highlight a tile from an unfound sequence, or Peek to reveal an entire pattern. Earn free hints by watching short videos.

🏆 Compete globally

Submit your scores to the Google Play Games leaderboard and climb both the score and furthest-round rankings.

✨ Clean & distraction-free

Interactive tutorial, sound effects and haptic feedback, no sign-up required. Free to play with optional ad removal.

Progressive difficulty
  • Rounds 1–2 · Easy — learn the basics with arithmetic and geometric patterns.
  • Rounds 3–4 · Medium — squares, primes, and Fibonacci sequences join the mix.
  • Rounds 5–7 · Hard — cubes, triangular numbers, and trickier combinations.
  • Round 8+ · Expert — power sequences, negative numbers, and dense boards.
